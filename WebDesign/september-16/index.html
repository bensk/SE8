Aim: What are the elements of design?

Do Now: Create a new Codepen page. Make an ordered list (ol tag) with ten items.

If you press ⌘/ you will comment your code. Comments are there for your references, and are not shown to the user.

    Good Design is:
    1. Innovative (tries something new. Technology alone is not enough)
    2. Useful (has a job)
    3. Aesthetic (Only well-made products are beautiful)
    4. Understandable (clear, at best, self-explanatory)
    5. Unobtrusive (doesn’t get in the way)
    6. Honest
    7. Long-lasting (is it well made? Is it something you’ll like in 6 months?)
    8. Thorough (even the parts you don’t see)
    9. Environmentally-friendly (minimizes resources)
    10. As little design as possible

ITERATE

To do something over again, possibly many times, in order to improve
